Our mission

Local 217 exists to protect skilled entertainment workers, strengthen the craft, and keep live events moving safely and professionally.

Represent skilled labor

We stand for fair treatment, safe working conditions, and professional standards for the people who build, run, and support live events.

Train the next generation

We invest in education, mentorship, and hands-on experience so members can grow their skills and carry the craft forward.

Serve the community

We support venues, productions, visiting locals, and community events with dependable crews who take pride in the work.
