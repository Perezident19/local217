How to join

Start with a simple interest form.

Workers interested in Local 217 can fill out the interest form, share their experience level, and ask questions about apprenticeship, calls, training, and expectations.

After that, the local can follow up with official next steps, orientation details, and any required membership or apprenticeship information.

Joining path

1. Submit the interest form.
2. Talk with a Local 217 representative.
3. Learn expectations, safety basics, and available training.
4. Stay connected through calendars, meetings, and calls.
